How it works

Three-step pipeline: analyse real data, suggest at onboarding, map services.

1
Analyse co-occurrence
Script processes 262k real SS business profiles. For each category, counts what % of those businesses also list under other categories. Filters: min 10 businesses, min 15% co-occurrence, max 5 suggestions.
2
Suggest during onboarding
After detecting "Plumber" from ABN/licence, shows: "Most plumbers also list under Handyman, Builder, Bathroom Reno." User toggles which apply — multi-select buttons, no typing needed.
3
Map extra services
Accepted categories get the same tiered service mapping as the primary trade — core services auto-added, specialist gaps asked about. Non-tiered categories go through LLM mapping.

Why this matters

📈

More leads for tradies

A plumber who also lists under Handyman and Bathroom Renovation sees 3x the lead volume. 83% of plumbers on the platform already do this — the wizard helps new signups catch up instantly.

🎯

Data-driven, not guesswork

Suggestions come from what 262k real businesses actually do — not what we think they should do. A 83% co-occurrence means "this is nearly universal for this trade".

⚡

Zero friction to accept

One tap on "Yes, all of these" or toggle individual categories. No typing, no searching through a dropdown of 200+ categories. The data picks the right 3-5 suggestions.

🔄

Regeneratable

As the business mix on SS changes, re-run the analysis script on a fresh export. New co-occurrence patterns automatically flow into onboarding suggestions.
