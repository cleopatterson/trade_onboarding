# Handyman — Subcategory Guide

## How to use this guide

When verifying a handyman's service offerings, use this guide to understand what each subcategory actually means in practice. This helps you ask smarter questions, spot gaps, and understand why a handyman might or might not offer certain services.

---

## Subcategory breakdown

| Subcategory | What it means in practice | Equipment/skill notes |
|-------------|---------------------------|----------------------|
| **Home Handiwork** | General handyman work — the catch-all | Most handymen offer this as their base service |
| **Odd Jobs** | Small miscellaneous tasks around the home | Broad category, often one-off tasks |
| **Property Maintenance** | Ongoing maintenance for homes or rental properties | May involve regular contracts, real estate work |
| **Flat Pack Assembly** | Assembling IKEA and other flat-pack furniture | Common job, requires tools and patience |
| **Furniture Assembly** | Assembling pre-made furniture (not flat-pack specific) | Similar to flat pack but broader |
| **Mounting Shelves** | Installing wall shelves, floating shelves | Requires knowledge of wall types (plaster, brick, stud) |
| **TV Mounting** | Wall-mounting TVs, hiding cables | Common job — needs bracket knowledge, cable management |
| **Patching Holes** | Fixing holes in walls — nail holes, larger damage | Plaster/gyp repair skills |
| **Door Repair** | Fixing doors — sticking, hinges, handles, frames | Common maintenance task |
| **Picture Hanging & Art Installation** | Hanging pictures, mirrors, artwork | Light items on hooks |
| **Picture & Mirror Hanging** | Similar to above but includes heavier mirrors | May need wall anchors for heavy items |
| **Picture Framing** | Custom framing of artwork and photos | Specialist skill — many handymen don't offer this |
| **Curtain Repair** | Fixing curtain rods, tracks, blinds | Includes roller blinds, venetians, curtain rails |
| **Lock Repair** | Repairing or replacing door locks, deadbolts | Not a full locksmith — basic lock replacement |
| **Tap repair and replacement** | Fixing or replacing taps, mixers | Basic plumbing — no licence needed for tap swaps in most states |
| **Gutter cleaning** | Cleaning gutters and downpipes | Outdoor work, ladder access required |
| **IKEA kitchen installation** | Installing IKEA flat-pack kitchens | More complex than standard flat pack — involves plumbing, electrical coordination |
| **Shade Sail Installation** | Installing shade sails over outdoor areas | Requires posts/fixings, tensioning knowledge |
| **Mailbox and letterboxes** | Installing or repairing mailboxes | Simple job, often combined with other work |
| **Shower Door Installation** | Installing shower screens and doors | Specialist — glass work, waterproofing awareness |
| **Shower Base Repair** | Repairing cracked or leaking shower bases | Specialist — involves waterproofing |
| **Broken sink repair and replacement** | Fixing or replacing sinks | Basic plumbing — similar to tap work |
| **Pool Table Repair** | Repairing pool tables — re-felting, levelling | Very specialist — niche market |

---

## Common patterns

**Home Handiwork + Odd Jobs + Property Maintenance** are the three baseline categories. Most handymen select all three. If they've only picked one, it's worth confirming they do the others.

**Assembly work** (Flat Pack, Furniture, IKEA Kitchen) is a staple of handyman work. IKEA kitchen installation is significantly more complex than standard flat pack — it involves coordinating plumbing and electrical, not just screwing panels together.

**Mounting & hanging** (Shelves, TV, Pictures) are core handyman services. Nearly all handymen do this. If they haven't selected TV Mounting specifically, ask — it's so common it may be assumed.

**Bathroom-adjacent work** (Shower Door, Shower Base, Broken Sink, Tap Repair) crosses into plumbing territory. Many handymen do basic plumbing tasks that don't require a licence — tap swaps, sink replacements. Shower work is more specialist due to waterproofing.

**Gutter cleaning** is outdoor maintenance work — requires ladder access and is seasonal. Not all handymen do roof-level work.

## Gaps to watch for

| If they've selected... | But not... | Ask about... |
|------------------------|------------|--------------|
| Home Handiwork | Property Maintenance | Whether they do regular maintenance contracts |
| Flat Pack Assembly | IKEA kitchen installation | Whether they do kitchen installs (much more complex) |
| Mounting Shelves | TV Mounting | Whether they mount TVs (very common handyman job) |
| Home Handiwork | Tap repair and replacement | Whether they do basic plumbing tasks |
| Home Handiwork | Gutter cleaning | Whether they do gutter/outdoor maintenance |
| Door Repair | Lock Repair | Whether they replace locks as well as fix doors |
| Assembly work | Shower Door Installation | Whether they do bathroom-related installs |
